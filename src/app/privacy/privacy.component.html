PRIVACY POLICY

            and products (collectively, “services”), such as personal appearance-related services and products; and, (2) “Providers,” who are those third parties who use MobileStyler to connect with the Users who would like to obtain the services and
            products which they provide to the public. Here, the term “customers” also includes prospective customers who are seeking to enter into a relationship with MobileStyler. MobileStyler does not provide any personal appearance services directly.
            MobileStyler acts to connect the providers of such services (“Providers”) with the consumers of such services (“Users”), and to facilitate an agreement between such parties for the provision of such services and goods at a mutually agreed
